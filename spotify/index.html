one-time setup

This manager runs entirely in your browser and talks to Spotify directly — it needs a free Client ID from your own Spotify app. Takes about 2 minutes:
  1. Open developer.spotify.com/dashboard and log in.
  2. Click Create app. Name it anything (e.g. "kataly manager").
  3. Set Redirect URI to exactly:
    https://kataly.cc/spotify/
  4. Under APIs, check Web API, save.
  5. Open the app → Settings → copy the Client ID.
  6. Paste it in spotify/index.html at SPOTIFY_CLIENT_ID, or tell kataly to add it.
Your app starts in Development mode, which is perfect for personal use — just add your own Spotify account under Settings → User Management.
